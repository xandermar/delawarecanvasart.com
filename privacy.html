Privacy

How Delaware Canvas Art collects, uses, and protects information when you visit or buy from our site.

Effective date

July 19, 2026

1. Overview

Delaware Canvas Art (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use delawarecanvasart.com (the “Site”), how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the Site or placing an order, you acknowledge this policy.

2. Information we collect

We may collect information you provide directly, such as:

  • Name, email address, and message content when you contact us through Support or email
  • Order-related details needed to fulfill a purchase (for example, shipping address and order number), which may be shared with us through Stripe after checkout
  • Optional information you include in a support request, such as an order number

We may also receive technical information automatically when you visit the Site, such as browser type, device type, and general usage data from your browser or hosting provider logs. The Site is a static website and does not use a custom account system or store payment card numbers on our servers.

3. Payments through Stripe

Purchases are processed by Stripe. When you check out, payment details (such as card numbers) are collected and processed by Stripe, not by Delaware Canvas Art. Stripe’s privacy practices apply to that processing. We may receive confirmation of payment, customer contact details, and shipping information needed to complete your order.

4. How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Fulfill and deliver orders
  • Respond to questions and support requests
  • Send order-related communications
  • Improve the Site and customer experience
  • Prevent fraud and enforce our Terms and Conditions

We do not sell your personal information.

5. Cookies and similar technologies

The Site itself is primarily static. Third-party services you interact with—such as Stripe checkout, fonts, or content delivery networks—may set cookies or similar technologies according to their own policies. Your browser settings control many cookies; blocking some may affect checkout or page features.

6. Sharing of information

We may share information with:

  • Stripe, for payment processing and checkout
  • Shipping and fulfillment partners, as needed to deliver your order
  • Service providers who help host or operate the Site, under obligations to protect your data
  • Legal authorities, when required by law or to protect our rights, customers, or safety

7. Data retention

We keep order and support-related information as long as needed to fulfill purchases, provide customer service, meet legal or accounting requirements, and resolve disputes. You may ask us about information we hold that relates to your inquiries or orders.

8. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information we receive. Payment card data is handled by Stripe using their security controls. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; please use a strong password for any Stripe or email account you use with us.

9. Children’s privacy

The Site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information to us, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

10. Your choices

You may contact us to update or correct information you have provided, ask questions about an order, or request that we delete support correspondence where we are not required to keep it. For payment or Stripe account data, you may also need to contact Stripe directly.

11. Third-party links

The Site may link to third-party services (including Stripe). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Review their policies before providing personal information.

12. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will change when updates are posted. Continued use of the Site after changes means you acknowledge the updated policy.
