The Studio

Coastal art with a local heart

Delaware Canvas Art captures the beauty of Sussex County through canvas prints inspired by Atlantic beaches, marsh wildlife, lighthouses, landscapes, and the towns that feel like home.

We believe walls should hold more than decoration—they should hold a place. Every print is chosen for light, mood, and memory: dune fences at golden hour, herons in tall grass, evening streets with salt in the air.

Pieces ship as gallery-wrapped canvas, ready to hang, with secure checkout through Stripe.

For locals, visitors & gifts

Whether you live here year-round or carry Delaware with you after a week at the beach, these prints are made to feel personal—beautiful décor for home, office, and the people you love.
